# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, review rigorously, and collaborate respectfully. Scientific disagreement is normal; personal attacks, harassment, and dismissive behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Critique ideas, data, and implementation, not people.
- Document assumptions and uncertainty explicitly.
- Respect reviewer effort and contributor time.
- Escalate concerns about data integrity, licensing, or reproducibility quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory conduct
- Misrepresentation of results or provenance
- Withholding known reproducibility issues
- Introducing unsupported scientific claims

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this policy.
